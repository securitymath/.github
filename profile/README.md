## Security Math

Smart contract security research founded on mathematical rigor.

We apply formal verification, invariant analysis, and adversarial modeling to protect DeFi protocols. Our focus areas:

- **ERC-4626 Vaults** — Share accounting, donation attacks, flash loan resistance
- **Lending Protocols** — Liquidation logic, oracle manipulation, interest rate models
- **DEX Architectures** — Concentrated liquidity, tick math, MEV resistance
- **Cross-chain Bridges** — Message verification, replay protection
